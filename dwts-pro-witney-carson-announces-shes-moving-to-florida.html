Dancing with the Stars pro dancer Witney Carson has been teasing a big announcement on social media, and on Thursday she finally shared the big news — she and her family are moving to Florida. The change has fans wondering about her future on the show.

DWTS Pro Witney Carson Is Moving to Florida

“No one said change was easy, but maybe that’s exactly what we need to grow,” Carson wrote on a video of her and her family moving into their new Florida home. “Embracing our next big adventure together as a family.”

Carson has been married to her husband, Carson McAllister, since 2016. They welcomed a son named Leo in 2021. Currently, Carson is pregnant with the couple’s second child together, which will be another boy. Last month, Carson teased that she had some big news to share with fans.

Some fans thought maybe Carson was moving to Los Angeles, where DWTS is filmed. The dancer previously lived in Utah, although she moved to California temporarily to compete on Season 31 of the show last year.

“I’m filled with lots of emotions, but I really am excited to have this adventure with my little family,” Carson wrote on her Instagram post, adding that she’ll continue to keep fans updated about life in Florida.

Fans Wonder About Carson’s Future on the Show

Fans on the DWTS Reddit had a lot to say about Carson’s big news. Some of them felt the announcement didn’t live up to the hype. Others wondered if the move means anything about Carson’s future on the show.

A few fans pointed out that Carson may not be competing on the upcoming Season 32, since she’s due to give birth in May, just a few months before the new season is expected to start. However, it remains to be seen if she’ll stick around for any future seasons.

As user @SaraJeanQueen pointed out, it may be more difficult for Carson to temporarily move her family to Los Angeles for the show once her son Leo starts school. We’ll just have to wait and see what her plans are for the future.
