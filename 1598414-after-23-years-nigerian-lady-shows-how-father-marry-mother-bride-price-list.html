After 23 Years, Nigerian Lady Shows How Much Her Father Used to Marry Her Mother in Bride Price List

 Posted on August 6, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  401 words  |  Patria Henriques
  • A Nigerian Lady shared a photo post on TikTok to show her followers a glimpse of how much was paid as bride price for her mother's hand in marriage
  • The lady shared a bride price list, which she said was 23 years old, dating back to 2001, when her father married her mum
  • The bride price list contained different items which her father took to his in-laws, with some valued at N50

A Nigerian lady sparked reactions on TikTok after she shared the bride price list given to her father when he got married.

The bride price list contained everything her father was asked to present to the in-laws as the traditional requirement for marriage.

In the list posted by Amarachi, some items cost as low as N50, while others cost as high as N12,000.

It is not clear the total amount her father spent, but it gave a glimpse of what the bride price list looked like years ago. She said her mother was from Abia state.
