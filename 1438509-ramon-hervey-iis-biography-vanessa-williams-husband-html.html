Educational background

The music executive and artist manager went to school in California. He went to Lompoc Junior High School before proceeding to Cabrillo Senior High School in 1968. After graduating from high school, he joined Whittier College to pursue a Bachelor of Arts degree. He graduated in 1972.

Career

Soon after graduating from college, the music executive and producer secured a job as a flight attendant. After a while, he realised that his passion was in the music industry, so he quit his Pan American Airlines job.

He became the publicist for Starlite Music Artistes, a position that allowed him to gain experience in the industry. He worked in this firm for a year before joining Hamlett Marsh Publishers as an editor.

In 1976, he bagged a job with Motown Records as a publicist. He was able to interact and work with renowned artists such as Diana Ross, Stevie Wonder, Marvin Gaye, and Smokey Robinson. He executed the artists' publicity campaigns so well that he earned their trust.

After Motown Records, he joined Rogers & Cowan as a publicist and writer. His demeanour and work ethic made him earn a promotion to become the music director and vice president of music and talent.

After gaining a lot of experience, he became a partner at The Gibson Group in the mid-1980s, where he worked with notable figures such as Rick James, Vanessa Williams, and Little Richard. He then founded Hervey & Company.

His company has worked with many famous and respected artists. Besides managing their talents, the company also organises live events and manages small independent record labels. The talent manager also works with different production studios as a public relations and marketing expert and consultant.
