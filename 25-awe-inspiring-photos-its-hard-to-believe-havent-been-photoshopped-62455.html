 Posted on August 14, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  209 words  |  Billy Koelling

The main secret of great photography is an ability to catch a good moment or see something others can’t see. Sometimes an event only lasts for a second. In order to capture that moment, photographers have even less time. Amazing photos that aren’t Photoshopped are always a combination of the right moment and a photographer’s ability to see an unusual perspective.

Bright Side put together a collection of 25 photos, for which the photographers happened to be in the right place at the right time.
