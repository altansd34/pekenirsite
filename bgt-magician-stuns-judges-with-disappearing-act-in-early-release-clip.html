Britain’s Got Talent has shared an early release clip from this week’s new episode, featuring an audition from magician Enzo Weyne that left the judges gobsmacked. Enzo made it seem like he was revealing the secret to his trick, before leaving us with even more questions.

Britain’s Got Talent Magician Enzo Weyne Shocks the Judges

Before starting his performance, Enzo told the judges and audience that he wanted to “test your powers of perception.” He sat in a chair on a raised platform, while his assistants covered him with a sheet. He stuck his hands through two holes to show that he was still there.

The audience was stunned when the sheet was ripped off, and Enzo was nowhere to be found. Then, a few seconds later, he appeared out of nowhere on the side of the stage. The judges watched with their mouths open in shock.

That’s when Enzo shared that he would be revealing the secret to his trick. He said he used the stage lighting to draw the audience’s attention and also hide things. He said he would do the trick again, this time wearing a reflective jumpsuit to make him more easily visible on stage.

We Still Don’t Know How the Act Was Done

Even though Enzo said he was revealing the secret to his trick, everyone ended up even more confused by the time he finished the second performance. As he sat in the chair again, he told the audience that there was a “moving plate on the back of the chair which allows me to escape at the very last second.”

He then appeared to demonstrate it, as a figure in a jumpsuit could be seen running from behind the chair to the side of the stage. However, that person then pointed to the opposite side of the stage, where Enzo suddenly appeared in his normal clothes.

The judges and audience looked on in awe, still scratching their heads about how Enzo accomplished the impressive disappearing act. We’ll find out what the judges had to say about the performance when BGT airs a new episode this Saturday night on ITV1 and STV.
