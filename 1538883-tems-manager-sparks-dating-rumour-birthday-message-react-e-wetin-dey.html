The manager of Nigerian international act Temilade Openiyi, popularly known as Tems, Muyiwa Awoniyi, has caused a buzz on social media with a message he penned to her as she clocked a new age on Sunday, June 11.

Taking to his official Twitter account, Muyiwa, also known as Donawon, poured out his heart to Tems, which sparked dating rumours.

Muyiwa described Tems as his twin, best friend, and all-around angel, as he said he was honoured to have her in his life.
