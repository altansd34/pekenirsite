Ecobank transfer code to other banks: list of USSD codes

 Posted on August 30, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1251 words  |  Martina Birk

The modern-day banking industry has made it extremely easy for customers to carry out transactions without going to the bank. Ecobank Nigeria has not been left behind when it comes to innovative solutions. One such solution is the Ecobank transfer code. Customers can pay bills, buy airtime, and transfer funds from their Ecobank account with the code.

The transfer code is particularly beneficial to people living in areas without internet access. It allows you to quickly and securely transfer funds from your Ecobank account to another Ecobank account or any other bank account in Nigeria.

What is the Ecobank transfer code?

The Ecobank short code is *326#. This USSD (Unstructured Supplementary Service Data) code gives customers numerous options related to using or transferring funds from their Ecobank account.

Upon dialling the code, one is directed to Ecobank's transaction platform, making it easy to access most of the bank's services without the need for physical presence at the banking hall.

What do I require to get a transfer code for Ecobank?

You will need a few things to register for the Ecobank mobile transfer code.

  • A mobile device for registration. (It does not need to be a smartphone; a feature phone will do just fine).
  • An account with Ecobank
  • A registered phone number with Ecobank linked to the account
  • An Ecobank debit card

How to sign up for the transfer code by yourself

Once you have all the requirements ready, here are the steps to get the transfer code.

  • On your mobile phone, dial *326#.
  • Ensure you use the number registered and linked to your bank account.
  • An on-screen prompt will ask you to select the debit card linked to your bank account.
  • Enter the required details and hit proceed.
  • You will be asked to verify your account ownership by entering some details.
  • Enter the necessary details and hit the submit button.
  • You will be asked to create a secret PIN (personal identification number) that you will use to authorize transactions.
  • Create a PIN you can remember easily.
  • Click on the submit button and wait for a confirmation message from Ecobank.
  • Once you receive an SMS saying your registration was successful, you are good to go.

    How to activate your Ecobank transfer code

    You have successfully signed up on the bank's USSD platform. Do you need to activate the service? No, you do not need to get stuck on how to activate Ecobank transfer code. Once the registration is successful, there are no additional activation procedures. The transfer code is ready to use.

    How to transfer money from Ecobank to another bank

    Ecobank transfer code to other banks enables you to easily carry out your bank transactions. It is a secure and efficient way of moving funds quickly and cost-effectively. Here is a look at the steps needed to transfer money from your account to another person's bank account using the USSD code.

  • Dial the shortcode *326# on your mobile device.
  • A list of options will appear; select 'transfer funds.'
  • Select the recipient's bank from the list of banks.
  • Input the recipient's bank account number.
  • The on-screen prompt will display the recipient's name. Confirm the name and proceed.
  • If the name is incorrect, cancel the transaction and start all over.
  • Press next and input the transaction password.
  • Input the PIN you created when signing up and click send.
  • Your transfer is done!

    How to get your Ecobank mobile transfer PIN

    In cases where you forget your mobile transfer pin, you can follow the steps below to retrieve it.

  • On your mobile phone, enter the Ecobank USSD code *326#.
  • Choose option 1, "My Account".
  • Then, option 4, "Pin reset with OTP".
  • A one-time password (OTP) will be sent to your mobile number via SMS.
  • Enter the OTP and select a new four-digit pin, preferably numbers you can easily remember.
  • Confirm your pin and press the send button.
  • If your pin reset is successful, you should receive an SMS notification.
