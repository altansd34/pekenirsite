Biography

The actress was born on 30th October 1970 in Brooklyn, New York, United States. How old is Nia Long? She is 50 years old as of 2020. She is the daughter of Talita Long, a teacher and printmaker and Doughtry Long, a high school teacher and poet.

Nia has an elder half-sister who goes by the name Sommore. She is a renowned comedian and actress in the United States.

Her parents divorced when she was only two years old. Nia Long young stayed with her mother, who relocated to Iowa City to take a course in fine arts. When Nia was seven years old, her mother moved to South Angeles with the plans of marrying her then-fiancé.

Unfortunately, the duo called off the engagement before tying the knot. Her mother opted to live in Angeles while her father stayed in Trenton, New Jersey.
