Competing on Dancing With The Stars as a pair, pro Britt Stewart and Daniel Durant manifested chemistry in the ballroom. It’s no shock that they developed love in the purest and genuine way possible, becoming life partners from being dancing mates.

Britt Stewart, Daniel Durant Relationship Timeline

September 2022: Stewart, Durant Meet on Dancing With The Stars

Britt Stewart and Daniel Durant became a dancing pair on DWTS Season 31 and became friends. Week after week, the two impressed the panel and the crowd with their routines. Due to their chemistry in the ballroom, some fans were rooting for the two to be together romantically.

November 2022: They Get Eliminated in the Semi-Finals

As the competition got tougher, Stewart and Durant were eliminated during the Semi-Finals. Yet, despite their Season 31 journey ending, the two continued to tighten their close ties. After the show, the duo managed to continue hosting a weekly Instagram Live with fans.

December 2022: Stewart Pens Heartfelt Message for Durant on His 33rd Birthday

On Christmas Day, the DWTS pro took the time to pen a birthday message for the actor. Alongside their adorable photo, she stated she’ll “always be grateful that @dancingwiththestars brought you into my life.” By this time, fans were speculating that the two were dating.

February 2023: The Couple Went Instagram Official

On Valentine’s Day, Stewart and Durant announced their relationship. Stewart posted a video of them dancing together, tagging the Michigan native alongside a heart emoji in the caption. To ignite the romance vibes, Stewart put a background music in the post, playing “Fall In Love With You” by Montell Fish. By the end of the month, the duo made their red carpet debut as a couple at the Los Angeles premiere of Creed III.

March 2023: They Spend Tropical Getaway in Maui

Walking away from the city for a while, the couple enjoyed a quick vacation at The Westin Maui Resort and Spa. On Instagram, Stewart posted a series of their sweets snaps. She also posted two videos recapping their Hawaii travel.

June 2023: Stewart, Durant Vacation at Sandals Royal Barbados

After their Maui trip, the couple then went to Sandals Royal Barbados to spend a romantic vacation. According to Stewart, such getaway gave them “the opportunity to experience so much together.” The two dined by the ocean, strolled along the beach, and bowled. 

August 2023: Stewart Hopes Durant will be Her “Forever Dance Partner”

During her interview with US Weekly, the DWTS pro stated that “life with Daniel is the most enjoyable thing.” She further stated that growing together and learning about each other are their focus in the relationship at the moment. When asked if the CODA star is her forever dance partner, Stewart answered that she hopes so. 

August 2023: They Announce That They’ve Moved In Together

Although the couple remains to keep their relationship private for the most part, they shared an update when they moved in together. Since they’ve moved in together, the couple shares glimpses into their home life together, through Instagram photos and videos.
