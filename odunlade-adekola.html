Career Timeline

  • 1996 - Odunlade Adekola began his acting career, the same year he joined the Association of Nigeria Theatre Arts practitioners
  • 2003 - He gained popularity for his lead role in Ishola Durojaye's movie, Asiri Gomina Wa
  • 2014 - Adekola won the Africa Movie Academy Award for the best actor of the year.
  • 2015 - He marked his entrance into the Nigerian music industry
  • 2018 - Won Best Actor in a Comedy at the Africa Magic Viewers’ Choice Awards
  • 2022 - Won Best Supporting Actor at the Africa Magic Viewers’ Choice Awards
  • 2022 - Won Most Popular Actor at the NET Honours

Relationship Timeline

The actor got married to Ruth Adekola in 2003, and they have four children together.

Family

Odunlade Adekola was born to Deaconess Adekola and Pastor Jimoh Odunlade. His father, Pastor B.A. Adekola, serves as a Servant of God at a congregation of the Christ Apostolic Church (C.AC) in Abeokuta. His parents are devoted clergypeople who hail from Ekiti, Nigeria

Net Worth

Odunlade Adekola has a number of brand ambassador deals under his belt, popular among them are Goldberg beer, Globacom Telecommunications, and RevolutionPlus Property Development Company.

He has an estimated net worth of around US$2.5 million. He is also the CEO of Odunlade Adekola Films Production which doubles as a film school.
