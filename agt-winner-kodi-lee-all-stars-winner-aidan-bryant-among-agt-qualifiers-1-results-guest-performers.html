It’s officially time for the first America’s Got Talent live results show of season 18. Following 11 live performances on Tuesday night, the show returns to NBC with results. Tonight, fans will find out which 2 performers will be heading into the Finale. Since the 11 acts will not be performing again tonight, there is a hefty list of guest performers for the episode.

AGT Superstars Cast Members Invited to Perform in Qualifiers 1 Results

To kick off the first week of the AGT results show, NBC has invited some of the cast of AGT Superstars, AGT’s hit Las Vegas show. The Las Vegas cast members appearing on AGT tonight are: AGT season 14 winner Kodi Lee, AGT All-Stars winner Aidan Bryant, Season 13 contestant Human Fountations, Season 12 contestant Light Balance, and Season 17 contestant The Pack Drumline.

For those who may not know, AGT Superstars is “a celebration of some of the best acts from the hit franchise America’s Got Talent.” The 75 minute show features a variety of performers, entertaining Wednesday through Sunday at the Luxor Hotel in Las Vegas. According to the show’s promotional video, the cast currently features Light Balance, Aidan Bryant, Kodi Lee, The Pack Drumline, Silhouettes, Human Fountations, Mervant Vera, and Celia Munoz.

Which Acts Will Go Through to The Finale?

Last night, AGT fans were able to see the first 11 contestants perform on the stage once again. Acts of the night included True Villains, Lambros Garcia, Mitch Rossell, Oleksandr Leshchenko, Adrian Stoica and Hurricane, Sainted, Brynn Cummings, John Wines, Maureen Langan, Lavender Darcangelo, and Ray Wold. As of writing, Golden Buzzer recipient Lavender Darcangelo has the most views on her second performance on YouTube. Behind Lavender is Brynn Cummings and Adrian Stoica and Hurricane, each bringing in around 80,000 views.

If the views are telling, Lavender, and Brynn may be the two contestants going through to the finale. To see which acts go through, fans should tune into America’s Got Talent on NBC from 8 p.m. ET to 9 p.m. ET to see the results.
