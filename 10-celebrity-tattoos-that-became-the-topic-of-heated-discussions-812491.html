10+ Celebrity Tattoos That Became the Topic of Heated Discussions

 Posted on August 19, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  653 words  |  Kelle Repass

Tattoos are becoming more and more popular, and celebrities seem to have fallen under the spell of this trend too. Some of them get a tattoo with a deeper meaning, while others just decorate their body this way. But whatever the reason, celebrity tattoos always attract a lot of attention and cause heated discussions among their fans and the media.

Jennifer Lopez and Ben Affleck

Soon after their reunion, which everyone in the world was probably talking about, Jennifer and Ben got matching tattoos.

Jennifer got an infinity symbol with her and Ben’s names inked on the side on her torso. It is her first tattoo, and she dedicated it to her husband. While Ben got a tattoo in the shape of 2 crossed arrows with the letters B and J.

Scarlett Johansson

VALERIE MACON / AFP / East News

In 2018, Scarlett appeared on the red carpet with a new, really big tattoo that depicted flowers and a lamb, and it caused some heated discussions. The actress still hasn’t revealed the meaning of this tattoo, but some fans believe that it’s dedicated to her daughter — Rose Dorothy Dauriac.

It seems that Scarlett loves this masterpiece on her back, so she tries to choose backless outfits to make sure everyone can see it.

Priyanka Chopra

Invision / Invision / East News

Priyanka has 3 tattoos: an inscription dedicated to her father on her hand and paws that are connected to her pet dogs on her ankle. The third tattoo was done in 2021, and it shows a world map. Usually, a world map tattoo implies that the wearer is a great traveler and loves to explore new places.

Olivia Wilde

Chris Pizzello / Invision / East News, BG029 / Associated Press / East News

The actress flaunts 2 delicate tattoos on her inner forearms, one of which she got just recently. The ink on her right forearm is the Taurus constellation, which is her son Otis’ zodiac sign. And the one on her left forearm depicts the Libra constellation, which is her daughter Daisy’s zodiac sign.

Sophie Turner

Invision / Invision / East News

Sophie has 11 tattoos, but all of them are quite small and not really noticeable. In March 2018, she got a tattoo in the shape of a rabbit inked on her upper arm. The actress hasn’t revealed the meaning of this tattoo, but some fans believe that it is dedicated to the day when she met Joe Jonas for the first time. It was at a Halloween party where Sophie was dressed as a rabbit.

Joe Jonas

Guerin Charles / ABACA / Abaca / East News

Joe showed off his newest tattoo on his social networks, which features the image of a woman staring through a peephole. Fans immediately decided that she looks like his wife, Sophie Turner. The singer, however, has remained silent about it.

Charlize Theron

JEAN-BAPTISTE LACROIX / AFP / East News

On the top of her right foot, the actress has tattooed a tiny blue blossoming flower. It is done in a Japanese style and symbolizes a peaceful attitude toward life.

Orlando Bloom

The actor accidentally got a misspelled tattoo in honor of his son Flynn. The dots and dashes on his arm stand for his son’s name spelled out in Morse code, but the first time around, the code spelled out “Frynn” instead of “Flynn.” Fortunately, Orlando quickly fixed the mistake with an extra dot.

Hilary Duff

In 2016, Hilary shared a photo of her new tattoo. It depicts 2 realistic roses. The actress hasn’t revealed its meaning, but some fans consider it one of the best celebrity tattoos ever.

Billie Eilish

The singer used to hide her tattoos, but in December 2021, she explained each of them and even showed off some of them. She also revealed her newest one — a little fairy on her hand. Billie says that this tattoo reminds her of the fairytales she read when she was a child.

Eva Mendes

Last year, Eva showed off her new tattoo that says, “de Gosling.” In Hispanic culture, the use of “de” unifies the woman’s last name with her husband’s after marriage. This tattoo became the topic of many heated discussions because Eva Mendes and Ryan Gosling didn’t announce their marriage, even though they’d been together for many years and have had 2 children together. So, fans and the media suggested that Ryan and Eva have probably gotten married.
