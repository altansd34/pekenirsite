After years of The Masked Singer judges and fans guessing that Darius Rucker is underneath one of the masks, he is finally headed to The Masked Singer. The Hootie & the Blowfish frontman will be joining Wednesday’s semi-finals as a guest judge for a night full of big reveals.

Rucker is a music legend in both the country and rock genres. Back in the first season of The Masked Singer, his name floated all over Twitter as many were convinced he was The Monster. The Monster turned out to be T-Pain, but fans held out hope that Rucker would one day come on The Masked Singer.

With hits like “For The First Time” and “Homegrown Honey,” the three-time Grammy winner is sure to bring his music expertise to the judging panel. Four contestants remain in the competition all vying for a chance to win the coveted Golden Mask Trophy. One of them will be unmasked this week ahead of the show’s finale episode.

Darius Rucker Joins ‘The Masked Singer’ for The Cluedle-Doo’s Big Reveal

The most exciting part about the upcoming episode of The Masked Singer is that The Cluedle-Doo will finally be revealed. This season, the show tried to do something a bit different by bringing in a side character to throw the competition for a loop. Instead of competing, The Cluedle-Doo has been taunting the judges and the audience with ambiguous clues about some of the contestants.

After his very first unexpected outing on The Masked Singer, superfans were convinced that Joel McHale is underneath the mask. McHale recently spoke out in an interview to further increase speculation. Based on the small clues given about The Cluedle-Doo, we’re pretty confident we know who is under the mask.
