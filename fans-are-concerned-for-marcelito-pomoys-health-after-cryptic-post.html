America’s Got Talent: The Champions singer Marcelito Pomoy posted a cryptic Instagram photo this week that had fans expressing concern for his health in the comments. Pomoy didn’t elaborate on what happened, leaving some people confused.

Fans Are Concerned About Marcelito Pomoy’s Health

Pomoy shared a photo on Instagram Tuesday in which the singer appears to be lying on a cot in a hospital while medical personnel attend to him. The photo’s caption consists of several praying-hands emojis.

Fans in the comments were obviously concerned about Pomoy’s health, with several people asking what happened. Others simply told Pomoy to get well soon and shared praying hands of their own. User @daviesmy wrote, “Mars you need to cut back on your hectic long concert tours, too stressful for you. We want you healthy and happy and well rested.”

Pomoy doesn’t seem to have given any recent indication of what could be wrong. He posted the same photo on his Facebook page with the same caption. Much like on Instagram, the comments don’t shed much light on what’s going on. Hopefully it’s nothing too serious, and the singer gets well soon.

Pomoy Competed on AGT: The Champions

Marcelito Pomoy won Pilipinas Got Talent in 2011. He went on to compete on the second season of AGT: The Champions in 2020. Pomoy wowed the judges and the Superfans with his ability to sing in both tenor and soprano voices on one song.

Pomoy made it to the Champions Finals, finishing the competition in fourth place. He was the highest-placing act during that season to be from a foreign show. Pomoy has continued to perform since his time on the show.

Last year, the singer announced that he would be touring in the United States. Last month, he performed with fellow AGT alum Peter Rosalita in Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates.
