 Posted on August 24, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  261 words  |  Billy Koelling

Being a celebrity is not easy. Most of us finish work at 6 p.m. and slip quietly into social life without thinking about work until we return to the office the next day. But celebrities are somehow forced to look perfect 24 hours a day. After all, they must be ready for the paparazzi or to shoot a new scene.

We did a little experiment and tried to imagine how stars would look like without cosmetic surgeries.
