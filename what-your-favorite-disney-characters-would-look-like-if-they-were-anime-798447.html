What Your Favorite Disney Characters Would Look Like If They Were Anime

 Posted on August 27, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  159 words  |  Kelle Repass

Maryam Safdar is a Pakistani artist who specializes in making all sorts of cartoons and illustrations. Even though she works with many different styles, we totally fell in love with her versions of the Disney princesses and other characters as anime.

This time, Bright Side wants to invite you to get to know these anime adaptations of our favorite female Disney characters. We hope you’ll enjoy them as much as we did.

1. Belle (Beauty and the Beast)

2. Snow White (Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs)

3. Cinderella (Cinderella)

4. Ariel (The Little Mermaid)

5. Aurora (Sleeping Beauty)

6. Mulan (Mulan)

7. Jasmine (Aladdin)

8. Rapunzel (Tangled)

9. Kida (Atlantis: The Lost Empire)

10. Merida (Brave)

11. Pocahontas (Pocahontas)

12. Alice (Alice in Wonderland)

13. Tiana (The Princess and the Frog)

14. Anna (Frozen)

15. Elsa (Frozen)

16. Tinker Bell (Peter Pan)

17. Jane (Tarzan)

18. Esmeralda (The Hunchback of Notre Dame)

19. Giselle (Enchanted)

20. Megara (Hercules)

21. Thumbelina (Thumbelina)

What other Disney characters would you like to see anime versions of? Let us know in the comments!
