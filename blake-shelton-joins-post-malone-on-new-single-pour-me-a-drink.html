Former The Voice coach Blake Shelton has collaborated with Post Malone on a new single called “Pour Me a Drink.” The singers recently teased the track for fans at CMA Fest, and it was officially released this week.

Blake Shelton, Post Malone Release New Song ‘Pour Me a Drink’

Post Malone has recently been venturing into the country genre. He kicked things off by releasing a duet with Morgan Wallen earlier this year called “I Had Some Help.” Now he’s teaming up with Blake Shelton on another country track.

On Friday, Malone and Shelton debuted “Pour Me a Drink,” the second single off Malone’s upcoming country album F-1 Trillion — out August 16. The pair previously performed the song in a surprise appearance earlier this month at CMA Fest.

“Somebody pour me a drink / Somebody bum me a smoke / I’m ’bout to get on a buzz / I’m ’bout to get on a roll,” they sing on the track. “Yeah, I’ve been breakin’ my back / Just keepin’ up with the Joneses / Y’all know what I mean? / Yeah, y’all know how it goes.”

Malone Also Teased a Music Video

Malone hyped up the new song on his Instagram page before its release. He even posted a clip that looks like it might be from a music video. He and Shelton can be seen performing “Pour Me a Drink” and having fun at an outdoor party.

Meanwhile, fans are sharing their love for the new single. One fan commented on YouTube, “F-1 Trillion will probably be my favorite country album of all time.” Another joked that they were “going to replay this one F-1 Trillion times.”

Shelton posted about the new release on his own Instagram account, prompting one fan to declare it the song of the summer. Others predicted that “Pour Me a Drink” will be a No. 1 hit. What do you think about the new song? Let us know in the comments below.
