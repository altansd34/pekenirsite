David Caruso bio

How old is David Caruso? David was born on January 7, 1956, in Queens, New York. This makes him 64 years old as of 2020. He has one known sibling whose name is Joyce Caruso.

He was born in Forest Hills, in the USA to Joan and Charles Caruso. His father was an editor, while his mother worked as a librarian.

Unfortunately, his parents separated. By the time of their divorce, David was reportedly 18 months old while his little sister was 6 months old.

David went on to study at Our Lady Queen of Martyrs Catholic School and later joined Archbishop Molloy High School, where he graduated before moving to work in theaters.

At first, he started working on small roles, before he got his major break in the TV series NYPD Blue. His role in the TV show saw him gain many followers hence becoming a star.
