I Love Being Pregnant So Much, Im Offering My Womb to Prospective Parents For $50K

 Posted on August 8, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  515 words  |  Patria Henriques

Pregnancy is a fantastic time when a baby grows inside a mom’s belly. Some moms really enjoy this time and feel a special bond with their baby-to-be. But there’s a mom who feels something even more special. She loves being pregnant and wants to help other people become parents.

The reason why she became a surrogate.

Latorre had a strong reason for becoming a surrogate. She went through a sad experience when she had a miscarriage before. This made her very sad and lonely. But she also felt thankful for her one child, knowing that some people can’t have babies at all.

In 2019, she had her second child, a son named Trey Junior. But after he was born and she stopped breastfeeding, she wanted to help another family have a baby. She tried to make a special connection with the family and be able to see the baby in the future. This was really important to her. She also wanted to help a mom who couldn’t have children herself.

She documented her surrogacy journey.

Latorre wanted to remember her surrogacy journey, so she took photos and videos. She got help from a group that finds families for surrogates, and they matched her with the right family. This was her first time being a surrogate, and the journey had a few steps.

She went to the doctor and did some blood tests to ensure she was healthy. Then, she signed a contract to agree to help the family have a baby. She also got some shots to help with the process, and they picked a day to put the baby inside her belly. For all this journey, she gets paid around $50,000.

She loves being pregnant and her family supports her.

Latorre really enjoys being pregnant, and her family thinks it’s great too. Her kids even know that she’s helping another couple by carrying their baby. She even made a TikTok saying, “When you love being pregnant but don’t want any more kids, you become a surrogate.” She finds pregnancy easy and likes how it feels. It’s a special thing for her.

In the comments, someone asked Latorre if her man allowed her to be a surrogate. Latorre replied, “He lets me make my own decisions regarding my body. That’s a real man.” She meant that her partner supports her choices and respects her decisions about what she does with her body. This shows how much they care about each other.

Some people leave rude comments in her social media.

Sometimes, mean comments show up on Latorre’s social media. People say things like, “You’re only being a surrogate for money,” or “Find a job,” and even “You’re doing this because you really need money.” These words can hurt, but Latorre stays strong and keeps doing what she believes is right. She knows her reasons and doesn’t let these comments bring her down.

She wants to keep helping other parents.

Latorre wants to keep helping more parents become moms and dads by carrying babies for them. She once said, “I’m a surrogate to change a family’s life.” She only chooses to be a surrogate for families who can’t have kids on their own. This makes her really happy because she knows she’s doing something extraordinary for them.
