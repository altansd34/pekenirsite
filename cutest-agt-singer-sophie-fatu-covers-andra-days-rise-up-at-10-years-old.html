Sophie Fatu may have only been five years old when she first stunned the judges of America’s Got Talent in 2018, but her talent and poise was that of a professional far beyond her years. Now at the impressive age of ten, Fatu has posted a new video on her YouTube channel, showcasing how her rare gifts have only grown stronger.

Sophie Fatu Showcases Drastic Vocal Development With “Rise Up” Cover

Fatu first wowed audiences with her singing skills on the Ellen DeGeneres Show at age five. She then auditioned for AGT with a performance of Frank Sinatra’s “My Way,” earning a unanimous yes from the judges. Though she was eliminated in Judge Cuts, Fatu has continued growing her vocal talents over the past five years.

Now at the mature age of ten, she shows off her impressive range and control with a piano-backed cover of Andra Day’s inspiring ballad “Rise Up.” Fatu’s smooth, soulful tone blends beautifully with Day’s anthem encouraging inner strength during trying times. Even at just ten years old, Fatu exhibits musicality well beyond her years.

On top of her vocals, Fatu also adeptly accompanies herself on piano throughout the emotional performance. Though she became known on AGT primarily as a powerhouse singer, her piano abilities demonstrate her continually expanding musical gifts.

Fans have been eager to hear new music from Fatu after she charmed the judges and audiences of AGT in 2018. As an artist of the young generation, it makes perfect sense for her to share her developing artistry on YouTube, where she currently has almost 40 thousand subscribers.

Sophie Fatu Is Working With a Vocal Coach

Without a doubt, Fatu has significantly leveled up her vocal talents since first auditioning for AGT five years ago. As evidenced in her recent emotionally driven cover of “Rise Up,” Fatu now exhibits pitch control, range, and artistic expression far beyond a typical child of her age.

Fatu’s remarkable progress stems partly from growing up, but a crucial factor unlocking her potential has been her vocal coach, Cody Jay Morgan.

On her popular Instagram account, Fatu frequently posts lessons and vocal exercises led by Morgan. In videos of their training sessions, Morgan can be seen patiently guiding Fatu through breathing exercises, range expansion drills, and controlled crescendos to bolster her technical skillset. Meanwhile, he nurtures her emotional connection to lyrics, praising the natural artistry she brings to each song.
