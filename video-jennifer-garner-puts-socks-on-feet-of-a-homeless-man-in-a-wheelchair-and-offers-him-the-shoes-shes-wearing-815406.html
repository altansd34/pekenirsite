VIDEO: Jennifer Garner Puts Socks on Feet of a Homeless Man in a Wheelchair, and Offers Him the Shoe

 Posted on August 28, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  272 words  |  Billy Koelling

This Sunday, a viral video featuring Jennifer Garner has deepened our admiration for this star. While she was out and about, fate brought her into contact with a man in a wheelchair. What she did next will bring tears to your eyes and remind you that the world needs more people like Jennifer.

She made someone’s day infinitely better.

KELA/Broadimage/Broad Image/East News, KELA/Broadimage/Broad Image/East News

The star is used to be regularly followed photographed by paparazzi, but this weekend, one photographer was able to catch an extra special and honest moment on camera.

Garner was casually going about with her day in California when she saw a homeless man in a wheelchair and started chatting with him and offered him a plastic bag filled with essential items.

Her kindness is never-ending.

However, at one point during their chat, the 51-year-old actress noticed that the man was barefoot. So, she stepped out of her car, crouched down in front of him, and started placing socks on his feet.

The 13 Going on 30 star then proceeded to remove her own sneakers in an attempt to provide them to him, but unfortunately, they were too small for him to wear.

MICHAEL TRAN/AFP/East News

This is when Garner spotted the paparazzo several feet away and headed straight towards him. She addressed him, asking, “What size feet do you have? Can I buy your shoes for him? He needs a shoe.”

The paparazzo inquired about the man’s shoe size, to which Garner responded, “10 and a half.” The man behind the lens then cheerfully replied, “Oh, I’m 11! Want me to give it to him? I can give it to him,” and indicated that the actress didn’t have to pay him. The mother-of-three then placed the black sneakers on the man’s feet, and the photographer mentioned that he had a blanket in his car and kindly offered it as well.
