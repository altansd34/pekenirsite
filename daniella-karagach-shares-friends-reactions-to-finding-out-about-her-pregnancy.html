Dancing with the Stars pro dancers Daniella Karagach and Pasha Pashkov are currently expecting their first child together. Recently, Karagach posted a video montage on Instagram showing friends’ reactions to learning she was pregnant.

Daniella Karagach Shares Friends’ Reactions to Pregnancy

Karagach’s video is set to Ben Platt’s song “Imagine.” It starts with the dancer’s own emotional reaction to seeing several positive pregnancy tests, before showing how other people responded to the exciting news.

The video includes appearances from a few familiar faces from Dancing with the Stars. That includes actress Kate Flannery, who was partnered with Pashkov in Season 28 of the show. When she hears Karagach mention a baby, she says incredulously, “Are you having a baby?”

Other reactions come from fellow pros Lindsay Arnold and Witney Carson, as well as Season 29 winner Kaitlyn Bristowe. But as Karagach wrote in the post’s caption, her favorite reaction was from her Season 30 partner Iman Shumpert.

“We have to take care of the baby, so we’re good,” she tells him casually over video chat. It takes Shumpert a second to understand what she’s telling him. Karagach laughs at his excited reaction.

The Couple Is Expecting a Baby Girl

Last November, Karagach and Pashkov announced they were expecting their first child together. Karagach said she learned she was pregnant the day of the DWTS Season 31 premiere, and she told Pashkov right away without planning anything.

In December, the couple revealed that they’re expecting a baby girl. They shared the news during Karagach’s 30th birthday party, which was attended by a number of famous faces from DWTS.

When sharing the news of their pregnancy, the couple revealed that Karagach is due to give birth to their daughter in May of this year. That means they only have a couple of months left to wait.

Karagach is one of several DWTS pros currently expecting a baby. Peta Murgatroyd, Lindsay Arnold, and Witney Carson are all currently pregnant. Meanwhile, Jenna Johnson recently gave birth to her first child with husband Val Chmerkovskiy.
